Sustainability

Our Materials

All of our materials are environmentally friendly, sourced directly from nature and carefully crafted to minimize our impact on the Earth.

Using special technology to transform into clothing is no longer news.

As more and more conscious consumers join the trend of sustainable shopping, we are proud to be part of recycling materials for our Mother Earth by utilizing our unique clothing making technology such as 3D knit with Zero Waste.

Our Sustainabale Threads

Our sustainable AMANN sewing threads and embroidery threads from the RECYCLED line are made of 100% high-quality recycled materials and have the same quality characteristics as the high-quality range products.

Sustainable Threads is an exclusively fair trade, eco-focused company promoting handmade lifestyle products, carefully co-designed with our artisan partners in India. Traditional art techniques are used to craft a collection with a modern sensibility.

Our Second Hand Products

Our pieces are created to last and live more than one life. When you want changes, we take them back to be worn and loved again. We take your clothes back. This is part of our commitment to a circular model.

By taking responsibility for the lifecycle of our garments, we are closing the gapes in the flawed apparel industry and setting a new standard for sustainability.

But we cannot achieve this alone. Every time you choose to return your clothes or shop on our second-hand platform, you are helping to disrupt the conventional cycle of consumption and create a future without waste.

Our Factory

With headquarter located in Italy, we believe that our factory is a great place to take care of our products and workers because we always strive to improve our working conditions and benefits for our employees to ensure their physical and mental health and safety.

Our factory works on reducing the impact on the environment as much as possible. We mainly rely on machines to manufacture our products.

Even though, our factory partially uses renewable energy, while unnecessary usage like lighting unless required, is turned off during the day to make sure no extra waste during the production.

Eco Friendly packaging

At EternalRetro, we strive to use recyclable, reusable and compostable packaging. Since reusable packaging, by minimizing damage, reducing the use of raw materials, extending its life and even reducing carbon emissions through efficient transportation, can often be the most sustainable option, we allow our customers to bring their unwanted items back to the store for recycling or reuse.
